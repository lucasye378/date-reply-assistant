# Competitor Research — AI Dating Text Assistants / Openers

## Rizzagic
- **Pricing:** Free trial not clearly stated; subscription tiers start at ~$5.25/week for monthly plan (~$22/month), weekly plan $7.99/week. 3 free AI photo generations on sign-up.
- **Acquisition:** SEO via tool listing sites (ListYourTool, navgood.com), TikTok/Instagram/X social presence (handle @rizzagicai). Word-of-mouth and community discussions.
- **Revenue model:** Subscription (weekly + monthly plans). Add-on AI photo generations at 60% off for Pro.
- **Revenue data:** No public MRR disclosed. A Reddit post about an AI dating assistant making $190k/month may reference this category but could be a different player (see RizzAI below).
- **Notes:** Targets Tinder, Bumble, Hinge. Claims 1.8x more matches with AI openers. Also offers AI bio generator, attractiveness test, photo rating, background check tools. Solid feature depth. Social media activity on X/TikTok/Instagram.

---

## YourMove AI
- **Pricing:** Freemium (7 messages/day free), Premium ~$17/month or ~$79/year (~$6.58/month). Some sources say starting at $4.99–$7.99/month for Pro. AI photos cost extra on top of subscription.
- **Acquisition:** App Store (iOS + Google Play), Google SEO (blog posts, reviews), media coverage (CBS, NPR, Washington Post, Forbes — article about AI finding users dates/relationships). Affiliate program (yourmove.tolt.io). TikTok and Instagram presence.
- **Revenue model:** Subscription (monthly + annual). Photo AI is a separate pay-per-use or add-on. 300,000+ registered users per their site.
- **Revenue data:** No public MRR disclosed. Featured in mainstream press which drives organic traffic. App has 4.3/5 Google Play rating from substantial volume.
- **Notes:** Founded by Dmitri Mirakyan. Chat assistant (generates reply options based on match's message) is the core product. Also offers profile writer, profile review, AI photos (quality criticized as "clearly AI generated"). Works across Tinder, Bumble, Hinge. Good SEO + PR flywheel.

## MatchGenius
- **Pricing:** Pricing page exists but requires login — no public pricing visible without account. Likely subscription model but specifics unknown.
- **Acquisition:** SEO (matchgenius.app ranks for relevant keywords). No significant social media or press presence found.
- **Revenue model:** Presumably subscription (AI-powered assistant for crafting engaging dating messages).
- **Revenue data:** None found.
- **Notes:** Minimal web presence. Pricing hidden behind login which suggests a paid product but makes competitive analysis difficult. Less established than Rizzagic or YourMove.

---

## ToolMesh Opener (by ToolMesh)
- **Pricing:** Free tool. No paid tier visible.
- **Acquisition:** Part of the ToolMesh platform (toolmesh.ai), which is a broader AI tools marketplace. ToolMesh itself is B2B/enterprise focused (AI agent control layer).
- **Revenue model:** Appears to be free consumer tool within a B2B platform — possibly lead generation for ToolMesh's enterprise product, or just a portfolio piece.
- **Revenue data:** None.
- **Notes:** Free, simple. Web-based. Just generates opening lines from a person's bio. No subscription, no apparent monetization. Not a real competitor in the revenue sense — more of a free feature within a platform.

## Key Insights
1. **No competitor publicly discloses revenue** — all are bootstrapped or under the radar.
2. **The $190k/month figure** from the Reddit SaaS post likely refers to one of these players. RizzAI is a candidate.
3. **App Store presence is a major acquisition channel** for YourMove — iOS + Android apps with 300k+ users.
4. **Freemium is the dominant model** — free tier with daily limits, paid subscription for unlimited.
5. **Pricing clusters around $5–$8/week or $60–$80/year** — similar to what a "dating coach" would cost.
6. **Photo AI is a differentiator** but also a liability (YourMove's photo feature gets poor reviews).
7. **Press coverage (CBS, WP, Forbes) drives organic growth** for YourMove — PR flywheel works in this space.
